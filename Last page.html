Partners

Develop a world of potential

Governments and organisations with the desire to create opportunities and transform the workforce can multiply their impact with Kalla Web Development Academy curriculum, training, and resources.

Governments

We partner with national and local Governments with a vision to leverage digital infrastructures to expand their economies and improve the lives of citizens.

Uganda

In Uganda, Kalla Web Development Academy is a critical component of Kampala's efforts to accelerate digital innovation and strengthen its workforce. Education of its citizens is the cornerstone of Kampala's State Digital Acceleration program. They plan to almost triple enrollment into Kalla Web Development Academy by 2022.

In 2016, kalla made a commitment to drive digital transformation in Soweto, Masaka by increasing digital skills and fostering an innovative technology startup community. Working with the Ministry of Education, we are exapanding the Kalla Web Development Academy program to equip the workforce to succeed in ana increasingly connected world, with a focus on digitization of the manufacturing sector and cybersecurity technologies.

Masaka District

The governing body of Masaka District, looked to Kalla to accelerate its digital agenda, foster economic growth, and drive innovation. To help the country build the workforce to realize this potential, we have already trained 60,000 new Kalla Web Development Academy students in digital skills including IoT, web development and cybersecurity.

International organisations

We work with nonprofits, nongovernmental organisations, and public-private partnerships by providing our curriculum, expertise, and technology to help multiply their efforts.

About Us

Kalla Web Development Academy is a global IT and cybersecurity educatication program that partners with learning institutions around the world to empower all people with career opportunities. It is Kalla's largest and longest-running Kalla Corporate Social Responsibility program.
